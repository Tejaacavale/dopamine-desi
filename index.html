Wait — why fake apps?

🍔

The craving, not the cost

Scrolling a menu, adding to cart and hitting "order" gives almost the same hit as real takeout — minus the ₹120 delivery fee, the guilt, and the calories.

🚬

Alone, together

The smoke-break site just shows how many strangers are online and lets them post one line: "made it through another day." That's the whole product. It works.

🧠

Burnout economics

An era of uncertainty + expensive everything = a generation that wants the feeling of a small win without the price tag. India has all the same pressures, turned up.
